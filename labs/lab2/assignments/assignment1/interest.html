About Fall Guys

Fall Guys: Ultimate Knockout is a 2020 platformer battle royale game developed by Mediatonic and published by Devolver Digital. It was announced at E3 in June 2019. The game drew inspiration from game shows like Takeshi's Castle, a Japanese gameplay from which fall guys idea was originated, and It's a Knockout, and children's playground games like tag and British bulldog

Up to 60 players compete in matches with battle royale-style gameplay. Players, represented as jellybean-like figures, move around a three-dimensional playing field, with additional moves such as jumping, grabbing, or diving to assist gameplay. The aim is to qualify for subsequent rounds by successfully completing each of the randomly selected mini-games
